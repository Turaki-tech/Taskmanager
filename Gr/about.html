About

Transport Services:is an organization for booking bus or taxis for a passenger to travel or transit from Gombe state to any other state within Nigeria.

we book bus for long journey transport, e.g from Gombe to kaduna, kano Lagos etc. And also short journey transport within Gombe state e.g from Gombe town to Dukku, Kaltingo, Kumo Kashere etc.

Also we offer taxi for internal journey from any where you are to any where want to go, e.g from main market to GRA, Federal Lowcost, Pantami, etc.

Our Services

We offer different types of services such as:

  • Long Journey
  • Short Journey
  • Taxis within Gombe town
